Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI detection work?

Our system uses advanced computer vision algorithms trained on thousands of plant images to identify disease patterns with high accuracy.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, the basic disease detection features are completely free for farmers. Premium features are available for large-scale enterprise use.

Which crops are supported?

Currently, we support major crops including Wheat, Rice, Corn, Potato, and Tomato. We are constantly adding more.

How do I contact support?

You can use the 'Contact Support' option in the menu or email us directly at support@sustainacrop.ai.
